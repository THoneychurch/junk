While you are going through what you would like on your order it is always handy to see what you already selected without having to remember it all, this is where the checkout window comes handy.

The checkout window is used to help you manage your order and provide the options to reset or proceed.


Here are some tips or using the checkout window:

- Checkout window will auto update each item you add to your order.
- Once your order has been processed you may save a copy of your receipt.
- You may close the checkout window and re-open it without closing the main application and your order will still be listed.
