Welcome to the official [MAKE YOUR OWN] PIZZA ORDER application, we are a local Australian business that has been operating in Melbourne for more than 10 Years.

This Program is dedicated for those who are bored of the same-old pizza shop menu choices and would like to have more control of what they want in their pizzas.


GETTING STARTED
Step I: Choosing A Size


(1A)

To begin the Journey of creating your own pizza you must choose your size, first 'click' on any of the three sizes (1A) in the application window to start the ordering process.



Step II: Select Your Topping


(1B)

Note: Pizza Bases Have Tomato Paste and Cheese Included.

Next is to choose from our range of toppings, click on the checkbox(s) to include to the pizza base. (1B)

If you wish to find out more about our toppings you can select 'Our Toppings' in the left-side panel to view more information.

Each topping has an additional price that is shown in the brackets, this price is then calculated in the total message at the bottom of the application. (1C)


(1C)



Step III: Adding Another Pizza

Once you have decided what you would like on your first pizza you can add another pizza to your order. Simply click on the 'Add Pizza' button left of the total message. (1C)

This will add your previous pizza to your order and reset the options, from here you will begin from STEP I and select another pizza size to add to the order.

You can add up to ten pizzas in a single order with a maximum total of $206.00 inc. delivery.



Step IV: Including Your Details


(1D)

If you're reading this it should mean that you are ready to order your delicious pizza, but before we can proceed you must select your order type. (1D)

Once chosen either Pickup or Delivery you can then proceed to filling out the form. Payment methods include Cash and EFT both in store, and deliveries.

For a more information on payment and ordering you can select the 'Payment' button on the left-side panel.

NOTE: For Pickups Please Allow Up To 30 Minutes For The Preparation Of Your Order.



Step V: Completing Your Order


(1E)

Now if you haven't already, click on the checkout button on the bottom right of the program (1A). This will open up your order in a separate window next to the main application window. (1E)

The list shown in the checkout box is your current order, this is the toppings and pizzas you have selected, and here you can double check your order.

More information and tips on the checkout window you can click the option 'Checkout' on the left-side panel.

Last step, click on the 'complete order' button on the bottom right of the checkout window and follow the prompts to process your order!

WE THANK YOU FOR CHOOSING US, AND HOPE YOU ENJOY YOUR OWN MADE PIZZA!
